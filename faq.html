Koluvu is a job portal that connects job seekers with employers by providing a seamless platform for job listings, applications, and hiring.

Employers post job openings, and job seekers can browse listings, apply for jobs, and track their applications. Employers can also search for candidates based on their profiles and qualifications.

Koluvu offers both free and premium plans. Job seekers can create profiles and apply for jobs for free. Employers can post a limited number of jobs for free, with premium options for enhanced visibility and additional features.

Try clearing your browser cache and cookies or accessing the site from a different browser. If the issue persists, contact our Support Team at support@koluvu.com.
