FAQ

What is Koluvu Job Portal?
Koluvu is an advanced job portal connecting job seekers with employers. It offers job search, AI-powered mock interviews, resume screening, applicant tracking, and more.
How do I register and apply for jobs?
Job seekers and employers can sign up by providing their details. To apply for jobs, search for a suitable role, click Apply Now, and upload your resume.
Is Koluvu free to use?
Basic job search and applications are free for job seekers. Employers can post a limited number of jobs for free, while advanced features like AI Resume Screening and Premium Listings require payment.
How does AI-powered resume screening and mock interviews work?
The AI reviews resumes to enhance visibility and helps candidates practice with automated mock interviews that provide feedback.
How can employers find and manage candidates?
Employers can post jobs, search the resume database, use AI Resume Screening, and track applications with the Applicant Tracking System (ATS).
Can I set up job alerts and track my applications?
Yes, job seekers can enable job alerts for notifications and track their job application status in their profile.
Is my personal data secure?
Koluvu follows strict data privacy policies to ensure user information is protected.
How does Koluvu ensure job quality?
Koluvu verifies job postings and employers to minimize fraudulent activities and ensure a high-quality job search experience.
What types of jobs are available on Koluvu?
Koluvu offers full-time, part-time, remote, internship, and walk-in job opportunities across various industries.
Does Koluvu offer career guidance?
Yes, Koluvu provides career tips, resume-building services, and interview preparation resources through its blog and AI tools.
How do I improve my chances of getting hired?
Keep your profile updated, upload a professional resume and cover letter, and apply to relevant jobs based on your skills and experience.
The website is not working properly. What should I do?
Try clearing your browser cache and cookies or accessing the site from a different browser. If the issue persists, contact our Support Team at support@koluvu.com.
